Jackie Wolf

BSIE, LMT, Owner

About

Jackie has been a licensed massage therapist since 2007. She ran a highly successful private practice in Kent, Ohio for 5 years. Recognizing there is a large demand for therapeutic work and medical massage, she founded The Stillpoint Therapeutic Massage Center in Hudson, Ohio where she now employs 6 additional licensed massage therapists.

Jackie built her business with a solid foundation. After earning her bachelors degree in Industrial Engineering and 20 years experience in corporate and entrepreneurial business, she graduated from the Ohio College of Massotherapy in 2006. Her engineering background brings a strong understanding of the body as a mechanical being. She understands energy and the loads and resistances you put on your body with respect to movement; this along with her analytical skills help identify pain patterns and enables her to work efficiently and yield quicker healing results.

She has completed over 150 additional hours of continuing education. Jackie is trained in Craniosacral Therapy, SomatoEmotional Release Therapy, Precision Neuromuscular Therapy, MyoFascial Release Therapy, Sports Massage, Kinesiology, Pelvic Dysfunctions, and Orthopedic Knee and Hip Dysfunctions. She has experience working with professional dance teams and athletes at professional, collegiate and high school levels. She also works closely with marathon and trail runners.

Israel Maldonado, Jr.

LMT

About

Israel graduated from Youngstown College of Massage Therapy and received his license from the Ohio Medical Board in 2000. He went on to attend The Utah College of Massage Therapy and graduated in 2003; this program consisted of an additional 815.5 hours of study and an additional 115.5 hours of hands on work. Here he was introduced to many modalities and concentrated on orthopedic massage. He also spent time studying in a Cadaver Lab.

Israel has spent most of his work experience doing clinical therapeutic massage in various physical therapy clinics, chiropractic offices and in a physician's diagonostic and rehabilitation center.

He is trained in orthopedic massage, sports massage, trigger point therapy, deep tissue therapy, swedish therapy, and craniosacral therapy. He is also a certified personal trainer.

Maude Johnson

LMT

About

Maude began her therapeutic massage studies in San Diego, CA at The International Professional School of Bodywork. It was there she became certified in Reflexology, Deep Tissue Massage and Swedish Massage. She moved back home to Ohio and worked on her Ohio requirements at The Ohio College of Massotherapy (OCM). She graduated from OCM and received her license from the Ohio Medical Board in 2012. At OCM she added to her credentials MyoFascial Release Therapy and Precision Neuromuscular Therapy.

Maude has a small private practice and currently works at the Crystal Clinic Orthopedic Center here in Hudson.
