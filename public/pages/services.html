Services

Therapeutic Massage

Outcome-based massage - the application of a specific treatment targeted to the specific problem(s) the patient presents with and are administered after a thorough assessment/evaluation by the massage therapist with specific outcomes being the basis for treatment.

Medical Massage

Therapeutic Massage Requiring a Diagnosis and Prescription from a medical doctor.

Sports Massage

Massage geared toward pain management and injury prevention of athletes. Various techniques are used; stretching is almost always used. The goal of the massage depends on the training phase and is focused on the specific muscles and injury of a sport.

Relaxation Massage

Massage that uses smooth, gliding strokes. The therapist moves at a slower pace and uses light pressure. The primary purpose will be to help you relax.
